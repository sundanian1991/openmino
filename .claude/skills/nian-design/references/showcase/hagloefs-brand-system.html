Core Tensions

Four dualisms define the brand. Each pair is not a conflict but a conversation.

Heritage ↔ Future

The wordmark plays on the tension between past and future — honoring 110 years while projecting a bold vision.

Organic ↔ Engineered

The symbol embodies legacy and craft — organic forms refined through precise, engineered geometry.

Nature ↔ Tech

The color palette creates nuance through contrast — earthy foundations meet bright, technical signals.

Fixed ↔ Fluid

Separating wordmark from symbol unlocks flexibility — breaking static lockups for dynamic expressions.
