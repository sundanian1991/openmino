Brand Strategy Review

2024-12-15 · Confidential

Executive Summary

This document outlines the key findings from our 2024 brand strategy review. The analysis covers market positioning, competitive landscape, and recommendations for the coming year.

Key Finding: Brand awareness increased by 15% in target demographics, driven by the sustainability campaign and new product launches.

Market Analysis

The outdoor apparel market continues to grow at 8.2% annually. Haglöfs maintains strong positioning in the premium segment, with particular strength in the Scandinavian market.
