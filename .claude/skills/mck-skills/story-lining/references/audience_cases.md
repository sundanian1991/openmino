# General Audience (观众) Case Study

## Scenario: Public Speech / Classic Presentation
**Audience**: People who have some interest but lack the specific context. Willing to listen to a story.
**Structure**: SC(Q)R

### Example breakdown (Lincoln's Gettysburg Address):
1. **S (Situation)**: "Four score and seven years ago our fathers brought forth on this continent, a new nation, conceived in Liberty..." (Establishes the founding background).
2. **C (Complication)**: "Now we are engaged in a great civil war, testing whether that nation, or any nation so conceived and so dedicated, can long endure." (Introduces the current crisis).
3. **Q (Question - Implied)**: Will the nation survive? How do we honor the fallen?
4. **R (Resolution)**: "...that we here highly resolve that these dead shall not have died in vain... and that government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth." (The core call to action and conclusion).
