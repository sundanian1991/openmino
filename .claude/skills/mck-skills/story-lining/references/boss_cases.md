# Big Boss (大老板) Case Study

## Scenario: Board Meeting / C-Level Briefing
**Audience**: Executives who value time, want actionable insights immediately, and understand the high-level business context.
**Structure**: RSC(Q)

### Example breakdown (McKinsey Board Presentation):
1. **R (Resolution - BLUF)**: Start immediately with the conclusion. *e.g., Page 1: "We are bankrupt." or "We must acquire Company X immediately to secure our supply chain."*
2. **S (Situation)**: Provide the baseline data. *e.g., "Over the past 10 years, we dominated the market..."*
3. **C (Complication)**: Introduce the trigger for the resolution. *e.g., "...but recent regulatory changes have opened the door to disruptive competitors."*
4. **Q (Question - Optional)**: *e.g., "How do we respond?" (Usually skipped as the R already answered it).*
