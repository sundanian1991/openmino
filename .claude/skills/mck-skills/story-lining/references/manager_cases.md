# Direct Manager (直属领导) Case Study

## Scenario: Team Sync / Manager Update
**Audience**: Direct supervisors or colleagues who are already fully immersed in the project context and just need the output to make a decision.
**Structure**: R (Resolution only)

### Example breakdown (Google Team Presentation):
Instead of the "Cook" approach (explaining the research methodology, data gathering, user interviews, and then finally the conclusion), use the "Waiter" approach.
1. **R (Resolution)**: *e.g., "The target user persona for our new feature is X, and we should implement Strategy Y to acquire them."*

*Note: Do not waste time recounting the S, C, or Q unless specifically asked, because your manager already knows why you are doing the project and what the challenges are. They just want the dish served.*
