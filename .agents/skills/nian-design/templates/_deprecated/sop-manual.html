Standard Operating Procedure

Product Onboarding
SOP

新产品上线的标准操作流程——从申请到上线的完整指南。

2024
Process Flow
操作流程
1
Product Application

Submit the product application form with all required information. The form must include product specifications, pricing, and target market details.

Required Fields
Product name, category, specifications, pricing, target market, sustainability certifications.
2
Design Review

Submit product imagery and packaging designs for brand compliance review. All materials must follow the brand guidelines.

AssetFormatRequirements
Product PhotosJPG, PNGMin 2000px, white background
Lifestyle PhotosJPG, PNGMin 2400px, outdoor context
Packaging DesignAI, PDFBrand colors, correct logo usage
Product DescriptionDOCXBrand voice, technical specs
3
Content Creation

Create product page content, marketing materials, and social media assets. All content must follow the brand voice and visual guidelines.

// Content checklist
□ Product page (title, description, specs)
□ Marketing images (product + lifestyle)
□ Social media assets (Instagram, Facebook)
□ Email newsletter content
□ Press release (if applicable)
4
Quality Assurance

Review all content for accuracy, brand compliance, and technical correctness. Verify pricing, specifications, and availability.

Common Issues
Incorrect pricing, missing specifications, brand voice inconsistency, low-resolution images.
5
Launch

Publish the product across all channels. Monitor initial performance and gather feedback.

Launch Checklist
Website product page live
Marketing emails sent
Social media posts scheduled
Press release distributed
Inventory confirmed
