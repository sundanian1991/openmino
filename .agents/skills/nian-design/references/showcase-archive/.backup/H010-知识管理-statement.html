Background & Context

The outdoor apparel industry is undergoing a significant transformation. Consumers are increasingly demanding sustainable materials, transparent supply chains, and performance-driven design.

"The brands that will succeed are those that can balance performance with responsibility — not choose one over the other."

Key Trends

1. Recycled Materials — By 2025, 60% of premium outdoor brands will use recycled materials in at least 50% of their product line.

2. Digital-First — Online sales now account for 35% of total revenue, up from 20% in 2020.

3. Direct-to-Consumer — Brands are investing in their own channels, reducing reliance on wholesale.

Implications for Haglöfs

For Haglöfs, this means doubling down on our sustainability initiatives while maintaining our commitment to technical excellence. The Nordiq Winter collection is a perfect example of this balance.
