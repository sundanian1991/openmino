Design Principles
The system follows five principles that guide every touchpoint.
01
Honest Craft

No shortcuts. Every element earns its place through function and purpose — the gear that goes where you go.

02
Earned Simplicity

Clarity through reduction. Complexity resolved, not hidden. The system breathes because it's been edited.

03
Nature's Contrast

Earthy foundations meet technical signals. The palette creates tension that mirrors the outdoors — calm base, vivid peaks.

04
Dynamic Structure

Flexible, not chaotic. The system adapts across touchpoints while maintaining a recognizable visual DNA.

05
Forward Heritage

110 years informs the future. History is a foundation, not a ceiling. The system respects legacy while pushing forward.
