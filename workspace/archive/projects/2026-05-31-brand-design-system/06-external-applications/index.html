Usage Guidelines
Logo: Minimum clear space = 1x logo height. Do not rotate, distort, or add effects.

Colors: Use brand colors only. Do not use red/green function colors in brand contexts.

Typography: Georgia for display, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for data. Never mix more than 3 families.

Photography: Outdoor, natural light, authentic moments. Nordic atmosphere. Product in context.

Co-branding Guidelines
Reference
Rules
1. Equal weight: Both logos must be the same visual weight. Do not make one larger than the other.

2. Clear separation: Minimum 2x logo height between brand marks. No overlapping.

3. Background: Use neutral backgrounds (white, light gray, dark gray). No busy patterns.

4. Approval: All co-branded materials must be approved by brand@haglofs.se before publication.
