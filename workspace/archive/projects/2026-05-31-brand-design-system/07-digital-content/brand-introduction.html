Core Values
核心价值
01
Craftsmanship
110 years of genuine craftsmanship. Every product is built to last, tested in the harshest conditions.
02
Sustainability
Committed to reducing environmental impact. Recycled materials, responsible sourcing, circular design.
03
Innovation
Pushing boundaries in outdoor technology. Gore-Tex, PrimaLoft, recycled polyamide — engineered for performance.
Heritage
品牌历史
1914
Wiktor Haglöf produces the first Haglöfs backpack in Dalarna, Sweden.
1930s
Expands from backpacks to a full range of outdoor equipment.
1980s
Introduces technical clothing with Gore-Tex membranes.
2000s
Expands internationally across Europe and Asia.
2020s
Launches sustainability initiative with 100% recycled materials.
